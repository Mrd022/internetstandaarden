Tinker Hatfield

A legendary figure in the world of sneaker design, is best known for his transformative impact on the athletic footwear industry. Joining Nike in 1981 as an architect, Hatfield's unique blend of design sensibility and engineering expertise led to a remarkable career that included the creation of some of the most iconic and groundbreaking sneakers in history. His innovative designs include the Air Max 1, Air Jordan III, and many other Nike classics, all of which pushed the boundaries of both aesthetics and technology. Tinker Hatfield's work not only elevated sneakers to the realm of high fashion but also revolutionized athletic performance footwear, leaving an indelible mark on both the fashion and sports industries. His legacy continues to inspire designers, athletes, and sneaker enthusiasts worldwide.

Jon Warren

Sergio Lozano is a highly acclaimed footwear designer known for his significant contributions to the world of sneakers. He is best known for creating one of Nike's most iconic and innovative designs, the Nike Air Max 95, which was released in 1995. Lozano's groundbreaking design featured a unique layered upper inspired by human anatomy and ribs, while the visible Air cushioning in the sole revolutionized sneaker technology. The Air Max 95 quickly became a cultural phenomenon, bridging the gap between performance and streetwear. Sergio Lozano's work on this iconic sneaker solidified his reputation as a visionary designer in the sneaker industry, and his legacy continues to influence contemporary sneaker design and fashion. Sergio Lozano's design achievements extend beyond the Air Max 95. He has played a pivotal role in shaping Nike's product innovation and design language. His portfolio includes contributions to other notable Nike sneakers and collaborations, showcasing his commitment to pushing the boundaries of footwear design.

Scott Hewett

Tobie Hatfield is a respected and innovative footwear designer known for his influential contributions to the sneaker industry. He is most renowned for his work with Nike, where he has played a pivotal role in designing performance and lifestyle sneakers. Tobie Hatfield's notable achievements include the development of the Nike Free technology, which revolutionized the concept of natural motion in running footwear. This innovation allowed the foot to move more naturally while providing essential cushioning and support. His commitment to merging technology and design has resulted in sneakers that not only excel in performance but also resonate with fashion-conscious consumers. In addition to his work on the Nike Free, Tobie Hatfield has been involved in the design of several iconic Nike sneakers, including models within the Air Max and Zoom Air families. His ability to blend cutting-edge technology with sleek and fashionable designs has made him a highly sought-after designer in the industry. Tobie Hatfield's legacy as a pioneer in sneaker design continues to inspire both athletes and sneaker enthusiasts, highlighting the essential role that innovative footwear design plays in the world of sports and fashion.

Steven Smith

Steven Smith is a revered figure in the world of sneaker design, known for his trailblazing contributions to the industry. With a career spanning several decades, Smith has played a significant role in the creation of some of the most iconic and influential sneakers in history. He is perhaps best known for his work with Reebok during the 1990s, where he designed the Reebok Instapump Fury, a groundbreaking sneaker known for its bold and futuristic design, featuring an innovative pump system for customized fit and comfort. The Instapump Fury's unique aesthetic and technological advancements made it a cultural icon, still celebrated by sneaker enthusiasts today. Smith's impact extends far beyond his work on the Instapump Fury. He has also contributed to the design of sneakers for other renowned brands, including New Balance and adidas. His design philosophy often blends functionality, comfort, and style, resulting in sneakers that not only excel in performance but also stand as fashion statements. Steven Smith's extensive portfolio and pioneering approach to sneaker design have solidified his status as a luminary in the industry, and his influence continues to shape the future of athletic footwear and streetwear fashion.
